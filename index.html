Sharpest thinkers

How it works

A council that learns your business.

No forms. Strategy consultant Peter van Rhoon conducts a 20-minute spoken intake about your business, your market, your friction points, and the decisions you're avoiding.

Based on that conversation, Peter selects your council: five expert seats chosen to counter your blind spots, speak your language, and challenge your strategy without sugarcoating.

Growth Intel doesn't reset. Every question you ask deepens the council's understanding. They track your pivots, remember past trade-offs, and sharpen with every conversation.

  • Task execution. The elders think with you. They don't do admin.
  • Legal or financial advice where liability applies.
  • A decision machine. The council sharpens your thinking. The call is always yours.

Your council over time

You start with five. It keeps pace with you.

Four times a year a new expert joins, added to your bench or swapped for one you have outgrown. Each is chosen for where you are then, and your monthly fee stays the same.

  1. Month 1

    Your five arrive

    After the intake, Peter picks five advisors for your situation. They know your market, your numbers, and what you are trying to do.

  2. Every quarter

    A new mind, for where you are now

    Four moves across the year. Hiring your first salesperson? A sales operator joins for it. Outgrown a seat? Trade it for the mind you need next.

  3. Always yours to shape

    You decide who sits

    Keep your five, swap a seat, or grow your active council to eight. It is your council, not a fixed roster.

  4. Any time

    Call in a council for one decision

    A pricing call, a key hire, a launch that has to land. Put together a temporary council just for that question, then go back to your regular five.

Five to start. Four moves a year to keep it sharp. Same price throughout.
